About Our Minecraft Server

You can get extra lives by doing cool stuff like riding a pig off a cliff or taming an ocelot. Every 10 advancements you do, you get one more life. That means you can live longer and still have stakes! But watch out, because if you use up all your lives, you will be kicked out of the server for good. So don't miss this chance to have the best Minecraft experience ever! Grab your pickaxe and join us today tomorrow someday!
